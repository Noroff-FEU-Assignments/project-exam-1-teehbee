About VisitValdres

Welcome to VisitValdres, the brainchild of a bunch of wanderlust-infected souls who found themselves drawn into the mystical allure of Valdres, Norway! Picture this: a group of friends huddled around a flickering campfire in the heart of Valdres, sharing stories of our epic escapades and misadventures. It was on one such night, amidst the enchanting Northern Lights and the soothing sounds of the fjords, that the idea of Valdres Ventures was born. We realized we had stumbled upon a gem hidden in the majestic landscapes of Norway, and it was our solemn duty (and absolute pleasure) to share its wonders with the world.

The genesis of this venture can be traced back to a comical incident involving a misread map and a rather enthusiastic attempt to communicate with sheep. Long story short, our attempt at finding a shortcut led us to an uncharted valley in Valdres, where we were welcomed by the friendliest locals, both human and woolly. That chance encounter ignited a passion within us – a passion to explore every nook and cranny of Valdres and to uncover its best-kept secrets. Armed with our cameras, notepads, and a knack for getting lost, we embarked on a mission to create a tourism blog that captures the essence of Valdres in all its glory.

ValdresValdres is not just a blog; it’s our love letter to this enchanting region. Through our quirky narratives, breathtaking photographs, and, of course, the occasional sheep joke, we aim to transport you to the heart of Valdres, allowing you to experience its beauty, culture, and, most importantly, its irresistible charm. So, buckle up and get ready for a virtual adventure that promises laughter, awe, and maybe a sheep pun or two. Join us as we unravel the wonders of Valdres, one blog post at a time, and let the journey begin!
